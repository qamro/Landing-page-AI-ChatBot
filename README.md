## 🧠 About the Project

**AutoReply** is an AI-powered chatbot agency that helps clothing e-commerce businesses automate their customer support and sales conversations — without lifting a finger.

Our landing page showcases the product, explains its value proposition, and converts visitors into clients. The site is live, deployed on **Vercel**, and built as a modern, fast, SEO-optimized frontend.

> 💡 **The problem we solve:** Clothing e-commerce store owners spend hours every day manually replying to repetitive customer questions on Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, and Telegram. AutoReply handles all of that — automatically, intelligently, 24/7.

---

## ✨ What AutoReply Does

AutoReply connects to your social media and messaging accounts, reads incoming customer messages, and responds with intelligent, context-aware replies — trained specifically for clothing e-commerce.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🧠 **AI-Powered Replies** | Understands customer intent and responds naturally |
| 📦 **Product Awareness** | Knows your catalog, sizes, colors, and availability |
| ⚡ **Instant Response** | Replies in seconds, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week |
| 🎯 **Sales-Focused** | Designed to convert inquiries into purchases |
| 🌍 **Multi-Platform** | One AI, all your messaging channels |
| 🔧 **Easy Setup** | No technical knowledge required |

## 🎯 Target Market

**Current focus: Clothing & Fashion E-Commerce**

AutoReply is purpose-built for online clothing stores — boutiques, streetwear brands, thrift shops, and fashion retailers who sell through social media and need to manage high volumes of:

- Size & availability questions
- Shipping & delivery inquiries
- Return & exchange requests
- Order tracking follow-ups
- Product recommendations
- Price & discount questions
